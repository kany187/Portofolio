BRAZZAVILLE

The capital of the Republic of the Congo, Brazzaville is a cosmopolitan and culturally diverse city that makes it very special and visted by lots of tourists. The town is very lively, with lots of things to do and see. It gets really crowded during the summertime. The city is among the top travel destination in Africa. The currency is the CFA franc (or XAF) and official languages include French and Lingala. Located near the Equator with a tropical climate. Brazzaville awaits new explorer and those who thrilled for the adventure.

Welcome

History

Founded in 1880, the city was named after the French explorer Pierre Savorgnon de Brazza. The local leader of the time was Mokoko with whom he signed a treaty of protection. Four years later, the city was built. Afterwards, it became the capital first of the French Congo and then from French Equatorial Africa.

Arts and Culture

With its picturesque monuments and sculptures, the city is the home of the most famous Africans painting school: Poto-Poto School of paintings. The Congolese culture is all about the music, traditional dance, and the "sapologie".

Food

Congolese food is one of the best culinary in the word. It is famous for being healthy and fresh. Also, there are a good variety of restaurants all over the cities with a different specialty.

'Mbote'

Linguala is the most popular native tongue behind the French which is the official language. An estimated 1.8 million people live in Brazzaville. The city has one international airport 'Maya-Maya' which is one of the main airports in the country. Railroad are also present, and they connect to other cities in the country like Pointe-Noire. And, the major mobile service are MTN and Airtel. Buy a sim card from one of these carriers to stay connected and available.

The beauty of the city is seen at night all along the bridge of peace. Moreover, from the bridge, you can also see the beautiful town of Kinshasa right across the Congolese river.
